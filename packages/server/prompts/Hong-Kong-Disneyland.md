#### Hong Kong Disneyland Information

Hong Kong Disneyland is located on Lantau Island, Hong Kong. It opened on September 12, 2005, making it the fifth Disney park worldwide and the second in Asia.

#### Location

The park is situated in Penny's Bay, Lantau Island, Hong Kong.

#### Getting There

Take the MTR to Sunny Bay Station, then transfer to the Disneyland Resort Line. The journey from Sunny Bay to Disneyland takes about 3 minutes. From central Hong Kong, the total travel time is approximately 30-60 minutes.

#### Park Hours

Operating hours vary by season and special events. The park typically opens around 10:00-10:30 AM and closes between 8:00-9:00 PM. I recommend checking the official website before your visit for accurate times.

#### Themed Lands

The park currently features 8 themed areas: Main Street, U.S.A., Fantasyland, Tomorrowland, Adventureland, Toy Story Land, Mystic Point, Grizzly Gulch, and World of Frozen (the world's first Frozen-themed land, opened in November 2023).
